Our Story

Connecting people with unforgettable experiences since 2018

Our History

Founded in 2018, EventHub began as a small startup with a big vision - to revolutionize how people discover and book events. What started as a local platform in Manila has now grown to become one of the Philippines' leading event booking services.

Over the years, we've partnered with thousands of event organizers and venues to bring our customers the best selection of concerts, workshops, sports events, and cultural experiences across the country.

Our Core Values

Customer Focus

We put our customers at the heart of everything we do, ensuring seamless experiences from discovery to booking.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform to deliver cutting-edge solutions for event discovery and booking.

Community

We believe in building connections - between attendees, organizers, and the broader event ecosystem.

Our Philosophy

At EventHub, we believe that live experiences have the power to transform lives. Our philosophy is built on three pillars:

  • Accessibility

    Making events discoverable and accessible to everyone, regardless of location or background.

  • Trust

    Building a platform where users can trust every event listing and transaction.

  • Joy

    Creating moments of joy by connecting people with experiences they'll love.

Our Mission

To revolutionize event discovery by creating a seamless platform that connects people with unforgettable experiences while empowering event organizers to reach their ideal audiences.

Our Vision

To become the leading event discovery platform in Southeast Asia, known for our innovative technology, exceptional user experience, and commitment to building vibrant event communities.
